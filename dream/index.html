Dream of life

I have lived through much, and now I think I have found what is needed for happiness.
A quiet secluded life in the country, with the possibility of being useful to people
to whom it is easy to do good, and who are not accustomed to have it done to them;
then work which one hopes may be of some use; then rest, nature, books, music, love
for one's neighbor - such is my idea of happiness. And then, on top of all that, you
for a mate, and children, perhaps - what more can the heart of a man desire?


- Leo Tolstoy, Family Happiness
